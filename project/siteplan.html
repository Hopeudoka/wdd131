This name was selected because this page is about a Latter-day Saints chapel in Akwa Ibom, Nigeria.

Site purpose

This site is about a Chapel building of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saint. It contains an interesting Homepage with engaging contents and facts about the building, a page for a gallery of photos of the building and a page inviting visitors to contact the missionaries or fill out a form requesting a visit.

Scenarios
